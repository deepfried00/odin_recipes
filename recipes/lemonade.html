Mint Lemonade Ingredients

Original recipe (1X) yields 6 servings

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Sugar: White sugar is beaten with the cream cheese for the sweet filling.
  • crushed ice

How to Make Mint Lemonade

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below —

  1. Combine mint and sugar in a large bowl. Stir with lemon juice. Cover and refrigerate lemonade, 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  2. Strain lemonade into a pitcher. Fill six (6- to 8-ounce) glasses with crushed ice; pour lemonade over ice.
